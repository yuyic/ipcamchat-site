Frequently Asked Questions

What makes this app different from existing security camera apps?

It’s the first AI app that lets you converse with your camera. Instead of just getting alerts and scrubbing footage, you ask questions in plain English like, “Show me everyone who came to the door,” and get instant video answers.

Can I customize what alerts I receive?

Yes! You can create custom alerts in plain language — like “Send me an alert when someone walks near my car ” or “Send me an alert when my kids come home.” The AI only notifies you when those events occur, reducing false alarms.

Do I need special hardware for the app to work?

Nope! It supports most ONVIF-compatible IP cameras and thousands of popular brands. You can either let the app automatically discover cameras or add them manually with an IP address.

Can I review past events?

Absolutely. All your data is securely stored in the cloud and is available for review for up to 30 days.

Is my footage safe and private?

Yes. All your video footage is stored and processed securely, following strict security measures. We never use your video data for training AI or any unrelated purposes — your privacy is fully protected.
